Lemon Cake

This recipe is close to my heart from my lovely sister. This ooey gooey lemon cake will melt in your mouth, even without any icing. A slight dusting of powdered sugar is all that's added to the top of this delectable delight.

The making of this cake is very simple. You won't regret trying it for any birthday party, christmas party, or any other situation. You might even make up an occasion just for an excuse to make it.

  • 1 1/2 cups extra-virgin olive oil, more for pan
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups plus 1 Tbsp. granulated sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups whole milk
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons, (optional extra zest for topping)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. Diamond Crystal or 1/2 tsp. Morton kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • Powdered sugar (for topping)

Down below are the step-by-step instructions for creating this cake. Before you get started, make sure you have all the ingredients out and ready to go. As well as all tools needed. Fill up the sink with HOT soapy water to help with clean up. Get your pans, whisks, spatulas, and measuring utensils ready! And bake your heart out!

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. Lightly oil baking pan with a pastry brush esspecially the sides. Line bottom of the pan with parchment paper and brush said parchment with oil. Whisk eggs, oil, and 1 1/4 cups sugar in a medium bowl until smooth. Add milk, vanilla, zest, and juice. Whisk to combine.
  2. In a separate bowl whisk flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda until combined. Make a well in the center and gradually pour egg mixture into well. Whisk, using circular motions to slowly incorporate egg mixture, until batter is mostly smooth. (some lumps are ok) Scrape batter into prepared pan and smooth surface. Sprinkle remaining 1 Tbsp. sugar over.
  3. Bake cake until top is golden brown and a tester inserted into the center comes out clean, should be about 30 minutes. Transfer pan to a wire rack and let cake cool before unmolding.
  4. Just before serving, dust cake with powdered sugar and top with more lemon zest if you have it

  5. Enjoy your creation!
