Grilled Chicken

Want something simple for the night that tastes amazing but isn't detrimental to your health? Are you an 80 year old man with Kidney issues?

Then this is the right spot! Let's get cooking!

Set up

First things first, you'll want to set out everything you need for the recipe.

  • Boneless, skinless, orgainic chicken breast
  • Non-stick pan
  • avocado oil
  • Bragg organic sprinkle
  • Sink full of HOT soapy water to help with clean up

Now that we've gotten everything set out we can get started. Be careful not to burn yourself on the pan.

Cooking Time!

  1. Get out a cutting board, and a knife, and cut up your chicken breast how you like.
  2. Get the avocado oil and put it on your non-stick pan and let it heat up the oil just enough.
  3. Coat the chicken in the Bragg orgainic sprinkle seasoning. A generous amount should be used. Don't let any chicken breast be seen through the seasoning. Don't worry, it's salt-free, your kidneys are ok.
  4. Be careful on this next part. It's a little trickey. Place the coated chicken into the frying pan. Oil may splash just a bit, so be careful.
  5. Cook chicken for 1 minute on high on one side until you can see it turning white.
  6. Flip the chicken over, and cover. Lower the heat to med-low checking every 5 minutes with a thermometer until internal tempurature reaches 165 degrees F.
  7. Once fully cooked, take chicken off the pan and place on a plate.
  8. To fully enjoy, use utensils like a british person, smiling all along as if you're correct about someting but won't say it because you're too polite.

Best served along side friends while teasting eachother. Get some Kombutcha, some asparagus, and some pasta, and you've got a great meal!

Enjoy!!!
